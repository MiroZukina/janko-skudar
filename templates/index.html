Guiding You Towards Clarity and Healing

My approach to therapy is warm, collaborative, and tailored to each person’s individual needs. I believe that many of our struggles develop as ways of coping with stress, difficult experiences, or emotional pain. In therapy, we work together to better understand these patterns, explore emotions in a safe and supportive space, and find healthier ways of relating to yourself and others.

My work is integrative, which means I draw from different therapeutic approaches depending on what feels most helpful for you. Whether you are struggling with anxiety, low mood, relationship difficulties, trauma, or feeling stuck in life, therapy can offer a space for reflection, understanding, and meaningful change.

About Me

Hello, I’m Janko Skudar, an integrative psychotherapist based in Ireland. My unique background gives me a deep understanding of personal change, resilience, and mental wellbeing.

I support adults facing anxiety, depression, trauma, relationship challenges, and life transitions. My goal is to create a warm, non-judgmental space where you can explore your experiences and work towards meaningful growth.

Online Therapy

Convenient and effective therapy from the comfort of your home.

No travel requirements, flexible timing options, and fully secure, private video sessions. It is perfectly suited for busy professional schedules, remote areas, or if you live abroad.

Clinical research shows that online therapy delivers the exact same positive clinical outcomes as traditional in-person sessions.
