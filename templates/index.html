About Me

Hello, I’m Janko Skudar, an integrative psychotherapist based in Ireland. My unique background gives me a deep understanding of personal change, resilience, and mental wellbeing.

I support adults facing anxiety, depression, trauma, relationship challenges, and life transitions. My goal is to create a warm, non-judgmental space where you can explore your experiences and work towards meaningful growth.
