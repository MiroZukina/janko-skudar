Guiding You Towards Clarity and Healing

My approach to therapy is warm, collaborative, and tailored to each person’s individual needs. I believe that many of our struggles develop as ways of coping with stress, difficult experiences, or emotional pain. In therapy, we work together to better understand these patterns, explore emotions in a safe and supportive space, and find healthier ways of relating to yourself and others.

My work is integrative, which means I draw from different therapeutic approaches depending on what feels most helpful for you. Whether you are struggling with anxiety, low mood, relationship difficulties, trauma, or feeling stuck in life, therapy can offer a space for reflection, understanding, and meaningful change.

About Me

Hello, I’m Janko Škudar, a fully accredited Integrative Psychotherapist based in Limerick, Ireland. I am an accredited member of the Irish Association for Counselling and Psychotherapy (IACP), and I strictly adhere to its professional and ethical standards.

I work with adults navigating a wide range of life challenges, including anxiety, depression, trauma, relationship difficulties, low self-esteem, stress, loss, and major life transitions. My primary goal is to provide a warm, supportive, and non-judgmental space where you can speak openly, gain greater self-understanding, and work towards meaningful, lasting change.

As an integrative psychotherapist, I draw on a range of therapeutic approaches rather than relying on a single model. This allows me to tailor therapy to your individual needs, personality, and circumstances. My work is informed by psychodynamic, humanistic, existential, CBT, gestalt, attachment-based, and trauma-informed perspectives, helping us explore both current difficulties and the deeper patterns that may be influencing them.
