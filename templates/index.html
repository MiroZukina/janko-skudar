About Me

Hello, I’m Janko Skudar. My journey from engineering to psychotherapy has given me a unique perspective on personal change, resilience, and mental health. I have trained and worked extensively in Ireland, supporting adults from diverse backgrounds with anxiety, depression, trauma, relationships, and addiction. My goal is to provide a safe, understanding, and reflective space where you can explore your inner world and find meaningful change.
