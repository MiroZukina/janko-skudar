About Me

Hello, I'm Janko Škudar, a fully accredited Integrative Psychotherapist based in Limerick, Ireland. I am an accredited member of the Irish Association for Counselling and Psychotherapy (IACP), and I strictly adhere to its professional and ethical standards.

I work with adults navigating a wide range of life challenges, including anxiety, depression, trauma, relationship difficulties, low self-esteem, stress, loss, and major life transitions.

As an integrative psychotherapist, I draw on a range of therapeutic approaches rather than relying on a single model. My work is informed by psychodynamic, humanistic, existential, CBT, gestalt, attachment-based, and trauma-informed perspectives.
